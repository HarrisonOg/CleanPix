# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2025-12-27

## Overview

This app is designed to be fully offline and privacy-respecting.
We do **not** collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Data Collection

- This app **does not collect any personal information**
- This app **does not track usage**
- This app **does not log activity**
- This app **does not use analytics**
- This app **does not require an account**

## Internet Usage

- This app **does not connect to the internet**
- No data is transmitted off your device
- No background network activity occurs

## Photos & Files

- Photos and images are accessed **only when you explicitly choose them**
- All image editing and metadata removal happens **entirely on your device**
- Edited images are saved **locally** to your device
- The app never uploads, backs up, or shares your images

## Data Sharing

- We **do not share data with third parties**
- We **do not sell data**
- We **do not transfer data**
- We **do not store data on servers**

## Children’s Privacy

This app does not collect any data and is safe for use by all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be included with the app and reflected in the “Last updated” date above.
